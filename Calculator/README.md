## Description:
Calculator that can perform following operations:

`+`, `-`, `*`, `/`, `1/x`, `x*x`, `sqrt(x)`, `%`

User can also use his keyboard to insert numbers and operations(watch the tooltips)

## Usage:
Just launch `Calculator.exe`
